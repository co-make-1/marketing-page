Making A More Cooperative Community

Welcome to Co-Make, a public forum app where you can post issues pertaining to your community's upkeep and unresolved issues! Through the app you can interact with other members of the community to fix outstanding issues such as potholes, blight, and other various problems that make your daily life more troublesome than it needs to be.

Together, we can fix anything.

The Problems We Are Solving

  • The disintegration in the relationship between local government and the people they represent.

  • The feeling of apathy many have towards local issues.

  • The inability of local government to reach and listen to their constituents effectively.

How it works


With Co-make you can make your voice heard on the issues you would like to see resolved in your community.

What you get


As well as seeing real change in your community you get rewards for being a positive community member in the form of points, freebies and local recognition.
